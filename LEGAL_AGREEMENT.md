# Rock&Roll UpsideDown — Co-Ownership Agreement

**Version 1.0 — March 2026**

This agreement is between Wolf Schram ("Co-Owner A") and the co-signing party ("Co-Owner B"), collectively "the Co-Owners."

## 1. Ownership
All intellectual property created within the rock-and-roll-upside-down repository is owned 50/50 by both Co-Owners. This includes code, architecture, designs, documentation, and any derivative works.

## 2. Decision Making
Major decisions — architecture changes, external partnerships, fundraising, spending over $500 — require agreement from both Co-Owners. Day-to-day development within personal feature branches does not require approval.

## 3. Revenue
All revenue generated by the platform shall be split 50/50 after agreed operating costs.

## 4. Contributions
All contributions to the `develop` or `main` branches become jointly owned automatically. Work in personal sandbox branches remains personal until merged to `develop`.

## 5. Protected Branches
The `main` branch requires approval from both Co-Owners for any merge. This is enforced via GitHub CODEOWNERS.

## 6. Dissolution
Requires mutual agreement or California arbitration as a last resort. Neither Co-Owner may unilaterally dissolve the partnership.

## 7. Third Parties
Any external partnership, investor agreement, or contractor engagement requires both signatures.

## 8. Digital Signing
By clicking "I agree as co-owner" in the RRUD Portal, each party digitally signs this agreement. Signatures are timestamped, IP-logged, and stored with a SHA-256 hash of this document at the time of signing.
