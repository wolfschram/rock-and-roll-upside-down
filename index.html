ROCK&ROLL UPSIDE DOWN — CO-OWNERSHIP AGREEMENT

Version 1.0 — March 2026

This agreement is between Wolf Schram ("Co-Owner A") and the undersigned ("Co-Owner B"), collectively "the Co-Owners."

1. Ownership. All intellectual property created within the rock-and-roll-upside-down repository is owned 50/50 by both Co-Owners. This includes code, architecture, designs, documentation, and any derivative works.

2. Decision Making. Major decisions — architecture changes, external partnerships, fundraising, spending — require agreement from both Co-Owners.

3. Revenue. All revenue generated by the platform shall be split 50/50 after agreed operating costs.

4. Contributions. All contributions to develop or main become jointly owned automatically. Sandbox work remains personal until merged.

5. Protected Branches. main requires approval from both Co-Owners. Enforced via GitHub CODEOWNERS.

6. Dissolution. Requires mutual agreement or California arbitration.

7. Third Parties. External partnerships, investors, or contractors require both signatures.
